Farm Fury - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how "Farm Fury — Last Beast Standing" ("the Game") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. The Game does not require account creation, login, or any personal data entry. All game progress is stored locally on your device only.

However, third-party services integrated into the Game may collect certain information automatically:

2.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (Ad ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

See Google's Privacy Policy.

2.2 Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

Crashlytics may collect:

  • Anonymous session ID (randomly generated, not linked to any identity)
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash stack traces and error logs

No personally identifiable information is collected.

See Firebase Privacy.

2.3 Google Analytics for Firebase

Analytics may collect:

  • Anonymous session ID
  • App events (session start, level complete, ad interactions)
  • Device type and OS version
  • Country and language settings

No personally identifiable information is collected. Data is stored in the EU.

3. How Information Is Used

  • Advertising: To display ads and measure performance
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve the Game
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix crashes

4. Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

On first launch, you choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. You can change this anytime in Settings > "Ad Preferences".

5. Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to any server. Uninstalling deletes all local data.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA).

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / LGPD)

You may have the right to: access, delete, opt out of personalized ads, portability, and rectification of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Device Permissions

  • INTERNET: Required for advertisements
  • AD_ID: Required by Google AdMob

Farm Fury - Kebijakan Privasi

Kami tidak mengumpulkan informasi pribadi secara langsung. Game tidak memerlukan pembuatan akun. Semua kemajuan disimpan secara lokal di perangkat Anda.

Tidak ada informasi pribadi yang dikumpulkan.

Farm Fury - Dasar Privasi

2.3 Google Analytics

  • ID sesi tanpa nama

  • Negara dan bahasa

Fatty Pig - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how "Fatty Pig — Last Oink Standing" ("the Game"), developed by the developer ("we", "us"), handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. The Game does not require account creation, login, or any personal data entry. All game progress is stored locally on your device only.

However, third-party services integrated into the Game may collect certain information automatically:

2.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (Ad ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from apps.

2.2 Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability and fix crashes. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Anonymous session ID (randomly generated, not linked to any account or identity)
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash stack traces and error logs

No personally identifiable information is collected. The anonymous session ID is used solely to group crash reports from the same device session. This data is used exclusively for improving app stability.

Processed by Google Firebase under Google's data processing terms: Firebase Privacy.

2.3 Google Analytics for Firebase (Analytics)

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand aggregate player behavior and improve the Game. Analytics may collect:

  • Anonymous session ID (same as above, randomly generated)
  • App events (session start, level start, level complete, ad interactions)
  • Device type and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Country and language settings

No personally identifiable information is collected. This data is used solely to understand aggregate player behavior and improve the Game. Analytics data is stored in the European Union.

Processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy. Players can opt out by adjusting their device's advertising settings.

3. How Information Is Used

  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve the Game
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix crashes and stability issues

4. Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

When you first launch the Game, you are asked to choose between personalized and non-personalized advertisements:

  • Personalized ads: AdMob uses your device data to show ads relevant to your interests
  • Non-personalized ads: Ads are shown based on contextual information only, not your personal data

You can change this preference at any time in the Game's settings under "Ad Preferences".

5. App Store Rating Prompt

The Game may occasionally ask you to rate it on the Google Play Store. This prompt is displayed based on local gameplay milestones only (number of sessions and levels completed). No data is collected, transmitted, or shared as part of this prompt. Tapping "Rate Now" opens the Google Play Store listing. You can permanently dismiss the prompt at any time.

6. Data Storage

Game progress (scores, unlocked characters, settings) is stored locally on your device in a configuration file. This data is never transmitted to any server. Uninstalling the Game deletes all local data.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). The Game uses Google AdMob which is configured for general audiences.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / LGPD)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data collected about you
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising at any time via the Game's settings
  • Right to Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. You can also reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

9. Device Permissions

The Game requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to load and display advertisements
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to check network connectivity before loading ads
  • AD_ID: Required by Google AdMob for advertising functionality
